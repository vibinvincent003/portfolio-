Business Career

Amazon

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos from his garage in Bellevue, Washington,
on July 5, 1994. Initially an online marketplace for books, it has expanded into a multitude of product categories, a strategy that has earned it the moniker The Everything Store.
It has multiple subsidiaries including Amazon Web Services (cloud computing), Zoox (autonomous vehicles), Kuiper Systems (satellite Internet), and Amazon Lab126 (computer hardware R&D).

Blue Orgin

Blue Origin Enterprises, L.P.[3] is an American aerospace manufacturer, defense contractor,[4][5] launch service provider and space technologies
company headquartered in Kent, Washington, United States.
The company makes rocket engines for United Launch Alliance (ULA) and manufactures their own rockets, spacecraft, satellites,[7] and heavy-lift launch vehicles.
The company is the second provider of lunar lander services for NASA's Artemis program and was awarded a $3.4 billion contract.

Bezos Expeditions

Bezos Expeditions is an American investment firm based in Mercer Island, Washington.[2] It serves as a family office for Amazon.com founder Jeff Bezos by managing his personal investments. The firm invests in early stage ventures, late stage ventures and seed stages of companies in many different sectors.[
